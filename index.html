HOW WE DO IT:

Through an extensive assessment process our project managers focus on designing a comprehensive set of recommendations to facilitate change. Reconstruction builds multi dimensional teams to enhance an individuals change process.

After a recommendation is submitted Reconstruction managers precisely pair necessary therapeutic personnel to a clients project. Every member of a team is part of a collaborative effort to maximize treatment effectiveness. These individuals set out to plan and coordinate a clients reconstructing. We believe in working with only the best and brightest individuals.

Following assessment and recommendation, the Reconstruction team goes to work on executing the plan accurately and effectively.
