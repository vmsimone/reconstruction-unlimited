WE HELP PEOPLE.

Reconstruction is a collective of mental health coaches dedicated to helping people reach their highest potential.

Reconstruction creates comprehensive solutions for anyone struggling with mental illness, substance use disorders, and behavioral issues—all built on the conviction that unlimited change is possible.

We are more than clinicians, coaches and counselors. The work we do is fuelled by a fundamental belief that we can do together what we cannot do alone. Reset your internal dialogue. Get free of self-defeating behaviors. Learn viable coping mechanisms. Opt out of the struggle. No matter where you are at, we meet you there and help create new solutions to old problems.

There is no project too big or too small, and we go to the farthest corners of the earth to help our clients get to where they want to be. We offer 24/7 support, interventions, quick moves and relocations. Reconstruction even designs customized, supportive ecosystems for our clients. At heart, Reconstruction Unlimited is about helping people change anything that’s not working.

How We Do It

It is our belief that anyone with a complex internal struggle deserves a tailored plan without limitations. We use tried and true methods as well as innovative problem solving, and an approach to intervention that is fuelled by creativity and ingenuity. We insist on working with the best and the brightest—each member of our team was selected because they possess uncommon skill and experience.

Our process is straightforward:
We begin with an extensive personal assessment. Our project managers then craft a bespoke set of recommendations designed to address a client’s needs holistically. We then assemble a multi-dimensional team of therapeutic personnel best suited to a client’s requirements. We believe that an approach informed by agility and insight helps us best respond to people— who are ever-changing, evolving and more than just their struggles.

We believe in creativity. It is a quality we possess in abundance. Creativity drives our process of designing unique solutions. Because we aim to serve the whole person, we are able to build solutions that acknowledge an individual’s struggle, while reinforcing their strengths. Reconstruction believes systemic change provides the best chance for continued holistic growth and development.

Why It Matters

Reconstruction was founded with the intention of assembling an unparalleled team of global counselors and therapeutic professionals. We bring to the table our combined experience, and a fiery commitment to finding solutions that work.

We do this work because we know that change is built from the ground up. We believe that everyone deserves a chance to live free of the things that have been holding them back.

We do it because we’ve found we can be whoever we want to be once we’ve freed ourselves of what restricts us.

We help people.
